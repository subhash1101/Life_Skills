# Prevention of Sexual Harassment

## What kinds of behaviour cause sexual harassment?

* Sexual jokes or comments
* Repeated asking out even after someone says No
* Unwanted touching
* Displaying sexual pictures, emails, or messages
* Using job power for personal benefit

---

## What would you do in case you face or witness any incident or repeated incidents of such behaviour?

* I will tell the harasser that the abusive behavior is unwelcome and ask the individual to stop.
* Keep a record of incidents (dates, times, locations, possible witnesses, what happened) for future proof.
* If, even after asking the harasser to stop the abusive behavior, the harassment continues, I would report the incident to higher authority.

---

## Explainers for different scenarios enacted by actors

* Artistic Freedom – Posting sexual pictures at work may lead to harassment if someone does not feel comfortable.
* You Gotta Keep Trying – Continuously asking someone out after they say “No” is harassment.
* Jokes & Emails – Sexual jokes at work are inappropriate even if the sender thinks they are funny.
* The Hugger – Physical contact like hugging is harassment if the other person does not like it.
* Twisted Words – Sexual jokes or double-meaning jokes at work are inappropriate.

---

## How to handle cases of harassment?

* I will tell the harasser that the abusive behavior is unwelcome and ask the individual to stop.
* Keep a record of incidents (dates, times, locations, possible witnesses, what happened) for future proof.
* If, even after asking the harasser to stop the abusive behavior, the harassment continues, I would report the incident to higher authority.

---

## How to behave appropriately?

* Be professional at work.
* Do not say, send, or do anything sexual.
* Respect personal boundaries.
* If your actions make someone uncomfortable, stop immediately.
