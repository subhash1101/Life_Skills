
## Question 1: What is Deep Work?
* Deep work means working with intense concentration.
* Deep work can also be described as giving full attention.
* It means avoiding distractions.

---

## Question 2: According to the author, how to do deep work properly, in a few points?
According to the author:
* Work for longer time periods.
* Don’t switch to different work suddenly.
* Avoid distractions like social media or news.

---

## Question 3: How can you implement the principles in your day-to-day life?
* Decide when I will work on a task.
* Create deadlines for my tasks.
* Try to finish tasks early to avoid last-minute pressure.
* Do deep work on the task I decide.

---

## Question 4: What are the dangers of social media, in brief?
* Social media is made in such a way that it can be addictive.
* Excess of it can damage a person’s career.
* It can make a person feel lonely.
* It breaks focus on the work we do and increases anxiety.
