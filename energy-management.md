# Energy Management

## Question 1: What are the activities you do that make you relax – Calm quadrant?

* Listening to soft music while sitting alone.
* Watching feel-good and comedy movies.
* Talking to friends and family.

---

## Question 2: When do you find yourself getting into the Stress quadrant?

* When tasks are still incomplete and the deadline is nearing.
* When trying to find errors and still not being able to find them.
---

## Question 3: How do you understand if you are in the Excitement quadrant?

* Time passes quickly because I am fully involved in the task.
* I feel highly energetic and motivated.

---

## Question 4: Paraphrase the “Sleep is Your Superpower” video in your own words

* Sleep is essential for brain function.
* Sleep is important both before and after studying to remember what we have learned.
* Poor sleep causes faster aging.
* Sleep also affects our DNA.
* Poor sleep causes memory decline and low testosterone.

---

## Question 5: What are some ideas that you can implement to sleep better?

* Avoiding naps during the day.
* Reducing the consumption of alcohol and caffeine.
* Going to bed and waking up at the same time every day.
* The human body needs to drop its core temperature by about two to three degrees Fahrenheit to initiate sleep.

---

## Question 6: Paraphrase the video – Brain Changing Benefits of Exercise.

* Exercise gives immediate improvements in mood, focus, and attention.
* Regular exercise physically increases the size and function of the brain.
* Exercise increases the growth of new brain cells.
* Physical activity has protective effects against aging.
* The benefits of exercise are both short-term and long-term.

---

## Question 7: What are some steps you can take to exercise more?

* Exercise 3–4 times a week for 30 minutes per day.
* Focus on aerobic exercise that raises your heart rate.
* Add simple activities like an extra walk around the block and using the stairs.
