# 📘 Communication & Listening – Notes and Answers

## 🔹 Question 1 – What are the steps/strategies to do Active Listening?

1. Listen carefully with full attention.
2. Do not interrupt the speaker.
3. Stay focused on the topic.
4. Show interest using small responses.
5. Use body language like nodding.
6. Repeat key points to confirm understanding.

---

## 🔹 Question 2 – According to Fisher's model, what are the key points of Reflective Listening? 
1. Listen carefully to the message.
2. Notice feelings and emotions.
3. Repeat in your own words.
4. Ask to confirm understanding.
5. Take notes during discussions.
6. Make sure everyone agrees on points.

---

## 🔹 Question 3 – What are the obstacles in your listening process?

1. Distractions like phone or noise.
2. Interrupting others.
3. Pre-judging people.
4. Lack of focus.
5. Strong emotions or stress.
6. Thinking about reply instead of listening.

---

## 🔹 Question 4 – What can you do to improve your listening?

1. Give full attention.
2. Avoid distractions.
3. Let others finish speaking.
4. Ask clear questions.
5. Take notes when needed.
6. Repeat important points.

---

## 🔹 Question 7 – When do you switch into Passive Aggressive (sarcasm/gossiping/taunts/silent treatment and others) communication styles in your day to day life?
